Download WORTH

Step 1: Set up your Android device first.

  1. Go to Settings (the gear icon, which might be in dock).
  2. Scroll to Personal.
  3. Tap Security.
  4. Scroll to Device Administration > Unknown Sources option
  5. Enable Allow installation of apps from unknown sources.
  6. Go back to Home

Step 2: Download and install.

  1. Open Google Chrome (the browser).
  2. Tap the Download WORTH button below.
  3. Wait while the package downloads. This may take a minute or two. You'll see the an icon in the upper left of the screen indicating that downloading is happening.
  4. The package will be in the Downloads folder. Tap the three dots near the top right of the screen and select Downloads.
  5. Tap the package.
  6. Choose Install.
  7. Wait while WORTH installs.
  8. Click Open (or, go to the Applications area, the icon in the dock with the six dots, and click the WORTH icon).

Step 3: Use WORTH. You're all set.
